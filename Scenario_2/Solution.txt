In Terminal:
1. ```git checkout your-branch```
    a. Add your changes to the branch 
1. ```git stage -A```
1. ```git commit -m "your message"```
1. `git checkout master`
1. `git pull` 
1. ```git rebase master``` // rebases the changes on your branch on top of master 
    a. ```git log``` // make sure your latest commits are on top of the changes in master 
1. ```git push``` // pushes your changes to remote branch 
1. Open pull request to merge `my-fork` with `master-your-name`
